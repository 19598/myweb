Archery Requirements

Bow Requirements:
  • Minimum draw weight of 40 lbs

Crossbow Requirements:
  • Minimum draw weight of 75 lbs

Arrow Requirements:
  • Arrow requires 2 cutting edges
  • Must have a width of atleast 3/4 inch
  • Mechanical and Expandable broadheads are allowed

Hunting Deer with Archery Equipment

Hunting Deer with a crossbow or recurve bow is fairly common. If you wish to hunt a deer, it is best to sit in a treestand and wait. Pick a spot in the woods that has a large opening that would allow you to shoot without your arrow hitting anything. It is best to leave salt blocks and corn in a place that you would like to shoot the deer. This way they think of where you want to shoot them as a place that they should go frequently. Make sure to practice shooting a lot so you can kill the deer quickly.

Turkey Hunting with Archery Equipment

Turkey hunting with a bow can be perfect for bringing home Thanksgiving dinner. Turkeys usually perch up in trees. If you find a tree full of turkeys and you're a good shoot, then that may be how you bag your turkey. If you don't find a turkey tree, it would probably be best to look in fields. Fields are where most turkeys are bagged, and that is where you will probably get yours. When you shoot a turkey, aim for the neck. Special arrow tips are made for turkey hunting, and are designed to decapitate them. If you need to improve your archery skills, check out my Archery Marksmanship Guide.
