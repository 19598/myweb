Ohio Trapping

Trapping in ohio is very useful for collecting pelts and getting rid of pests. With the exception of the River Otter, there is no seasonal bag limit for any trapping animal in Ohio. If you are trying to get rid of pests, try and place traps in areas where they are being an annoyance. If raccoons are in your chicken coop, put a trap by your chicken coop. If you want to catch a muskrat for its pelt, it is best to place traps by rivers and ponds. Look for muskrat holes or areas that look heavily traveled.
